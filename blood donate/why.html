WHY DONATE

Emergency situations (road accidents, women having difficult childbirth,
hemorrhages during surgical operations, etc.)
which may require the provision of large volumes of blood products;


Needs outside of emergencies, particularly for patients with
blood diseases such as sickle cell patients who require
regular blood donations throughout their lives, or in cases of
serious illnesses such as cancer.
